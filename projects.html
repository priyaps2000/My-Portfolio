Efficient Activity Recognition Using Wi-Fi CSI

Have you ever thought that a Broadcom WiFi chip could recognize what you are doing inside a room using WiFi signals from your home router?

Yes!, It doesnt require any wearables, sensors or camera. We apply machine learning techniques on channel state information(CSI) from WiFi signals to identify human gestures.
